Build Realtime Apps now! Jet uses fast push technologies and differential updates to keep all clients in sync. It is very easy to use and features powerful query filters, sorting rules and a Web Inspector.

Jet is suitable for IoT Apps , Games, Collaborative Apps or anything else where things are in flux and fast communication is required.

Due to its efficient design, Jet Servers run very nicely on systems with rather limited ressources like the RaspberryPi.
